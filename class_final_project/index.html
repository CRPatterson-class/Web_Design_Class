Welcome to Fields of Misteria - An Overview From a Fan

A cozy farming RPG filled with magic, mystery, and community-- where your choices shape the town's future.

Games Introduction

The town of Mistria has fallen into hardships after a disastrous eathquake that affected the town, its fields untended and its people losing hope. Adeline and her brother Eiland set out to find someone who can help restore what was lost, hinting at the vacant south farm where a newcomer to the town could make their home. That someone is you, ready to bring life, magic, and community back to the village.

What Field Of Misteria Has To Offer

Here is a brief overview of the main mechanics Field of Misteria has:

Farming

Bring Mistria’s soil back to life by planting crops, tending fields, and caring for animals. With each season offering something new, farming becomes the heart of your daily routine and the foundation for the town’s revival.

Exploration

Beyond the village lie forests, caves, and mysterious ruins waiting to be uncovered. Every path leads to discoveries—whether it’s resources to gather, secrets to learn, or new places to chart.

Treasure Hunting

Scattered throughout Mistria are hidden treasures that reward curiosity and persistence. From rare materials to special items, each find brings new excitement to your journey.

Fighting

Danger lurks in the wilds, and monsters guard what the town needs to thrive. Combat blends simple tools with growing magical abilities, letting you defend yourself and protect the community.

Fishing

Calm waters provide both relaxation and reward. Cast your line in rivers, lakes, and along the shore to catch a wide variety of fish—some common, others surprisingly rare.

Relationships

The heart of Mistria is its people, each with unique stories and personalities. Build friendships, earn trust, and, if you wish, pursue romance that blossoms into something lasting.
